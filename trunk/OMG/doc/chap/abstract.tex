\begin{cabstract}

	由于立体电影和立体电视节目的日益普及，将普通的视频转换为立体视频也成为了一个极其具有实用意义的研究课题。
而立体视频播放设备的普及，使得普通用户也可以使用诸如电视，手机等设备观看立体视频。但是另一方面立体视频的资源是很缺乏的，
所以我们希望能够开发出普通用户也可以使用的转换工具，让人人都可以制作自己的立体视频。

在毕业设计的过程中，我以已有的标注模型和图像分割算法为基础，完成了基于触摸屏的图像三维信息标注工具的设计，并且基于Android
移动平台开发了一款图像标注工具。

\end{cabstract}

\begin{eabstract}

Since the popularization of stereo video, it has been meaningful to make stereo video from traditional video. The popularization of the stereoscopic device make it possible for domestic consumers to watch stereo video through devices such as television or cellphone. But on the other side, we are lack of stereo video. So we wants to develop a tool for domestic consumers to make stereo video and make it possible for them to make stereo video by themselves.

I design an annotation tool based on touch screen and develop it on Android mobile device as my graduation project based on existed label model and graph cut algorithms.

\end{eabstract}
